# 🚗 Decentralized Ride-Sharing Review System

Welcome to the most trustworthy way to review ride-sharing experiences on the blockchain! This project uses the Stacks blockchain to ensure transparent, tamper-proof ratings for drivers and passengers.

## ✨ Features

🔒 Submit immutable reviews for rides  
🛡️ Verify user identities to prevent fake accounts  
⭐ Aggregate fair, transparent ratings  
🚫 Prevent spam or duplicate reviews  
📊 Verify review authenticity instantly  
💰 Earn tokens for honest reviews  

## 🛠 How It Works

**For Passengers**  
- Complete a ride and submit a rating with an optional comment  
- Verify your identity to ensure authenticity  
- Earn tokens for valid reviews  

**For Drivers**  
- Submit ratings for passengers after rides  
- View your aggregated rating history  
- Dispute unfair reviews  

**For Verifiers**  
- Check review details and authenticity on the blockchain  
- Confirm user ratings and history  

That's it! Transparent, blockchain-secured reviews
